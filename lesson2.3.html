Lesson 2.3 Headlines, Lines, and Comments

Headings

HTML has six levels of headings, from <h1>: (most important) to <h6>: (least important)

<html>
 <body>
   <h1>This is heading 1</h1>
   <h2>This is heading 2</h2>
   <h3>This is heading 3</h3>
   <h4>This is heading 4</h4>
   <h5>This is heading 5</h5>
   <h6>This is heading 6</h6>
 </body>
</html>

Horizontal Lines

Use the <hr> tag to insert a horizontal line in your document.

<html>
<body>
  <p>This is a paragraph.</p>
  <hr />
  <p>This is another paragraph.</p>
</body>
</html>

Comments

Comments are notes you can add to your HTML code that are not shown on the webpage. They help you remember what parts of your code do. Use <- Your Comment here ->
